# Cast — the project's Who

Personas: hypothetical archetypal actors, drawn from research, used to keep the
work grounded in who it serves (Powell, Keenan & McDaid 2007, after Cooper &
Reimann's Interaction Design).

**Mara and Wren are examples** — the operators of any agentic host: the human
who develops software and the agentic LLM who develops software. They illustrate
the method, not your project.

**Each project builds at least one persona of its own, by discussion** — the
operator and the agent eliciting the actual users of the software under
development (allium's `elicit`). Add them here. See
[applying-personas.md](applying-personas.md) for the process.

(If your software under development is itself an agentic host, Mara and Wren may
double as your real cast — the example sits at the meta level it was drawn from.)
